Tanzania earthquake kills 11, injures nearly 200 by Faith Karimi, CNN. Updated 3:26 AM ET, Sun September 11, 2016.

(CNN)A 5.9-magnitude earthquake hit northwestern Tanzania, killing 11 people and leaving dozens injured,
state media reported. At least 192 people were injured when the quake struck near Nsunga town Saturday, according to
Daily News Tanzania. It hit near the shore of Lake Victoria, the US Geological Survey said. The tremors rippled as far
as western Kenya and parts of Uganda, both of which share the waters of Lake Victoria.
